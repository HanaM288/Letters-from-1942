Yours very sinerely,...
A Digital Compilation of Letters from 1942

Home | About | Gallery | Transcriptions | Metadata |







After meeting in Scotland in the midst of World War II, James and Doreen began writing letters to each other. Their correspondence would continue for many years, often writing to each other several times a week. This digitisation project takes us back to 1942 and allows us to learn about the daily lives of ordinary people during mid-century wartime.

Authors:
James Mills & Doreen Mills (nee Brown)

Transcription by:
Hannah Mills & Jessica Pettit
