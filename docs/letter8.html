Letter 8 - August 31st

"Edale",
2, Cranbourne Road,
Chortlton.cum.Hardy,
Manchester. 21.
Thursday, 31st

My dear Jim,

I really must write and thank you
very much for the wonderful times we
have had together this weekend. I have
enjoyed every moment of it.

You will be glad to know that
I caught the eighty bus all right, and
got home about 10-30.

Don’t bother to get your cape yet, as
Dad says you can have his coupons



and I will try and see whether
Brown Bros. have any.

I will call in for the book
to-morrow.

I am writing this in a hurry
before my breakfast, so I haven’t
very much time.

All my Love, Jim.
Doreen.
